ShipCube is a logistics and supply chain management company providing reliable domestic and international parcel delivery, freight forwarding, and warehouse automation.

Founded: 2016
Headquarters: Mumbai, India
Employees: 500+
Operational Hubs: Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, and Kolkata.

ShipCube offers integrated logistics services for retail, e-commerce, and enterprise clients. Our services include:
- Domestic & International Shipping
- Real-time Tracking and Notifications
- Reverse Logistics and Returns Management
- API-based Integration for Business Clients
- Warehouse Storage and Inventory Optimization

Mission: To simplify global delivery through intelligent automation.
Vision: To make logistics transparent, predictable, and human-friendly.

Departments:
1. Operations – manages shipments, pickups, and route optimization.
2. Finance – handles billing, invoicing, and claims.
3. Customer Care – manages support and client communication.
4. Technical Team – maintains systems, integrations, and AI automation.
