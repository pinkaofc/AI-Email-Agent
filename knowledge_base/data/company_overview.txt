---

## About ShipCube
ShipCube is a leading logistics and supply chain management company offering **domestic and international parcel delivery**, **freight forwarding**, and **warehouse automation**.  

Founded in 2016, ShipCube focuses on providing **reliable, technology-driven logistics solutions** that simplify delivery operations for individuals, small businesses, and large enterprises. The company has built a robust infrastructure across major Indian cities, supported by intelligent automation and data-driven route optimization.

---

## Our Services
ShipCube provides an integrated logistics ecosystem designed to meet diverse client needs across retail, e-commerce, and enterprise sectors.

### 1. Domestic & International Shipping
Fast, reliable, and trackable delivery across India and to major international destinations, with both standard and express options.

### 2. Real-Time Tracking and Notifications
Advanced tracking systems with real-time status updates via email, SMS, and chatbot integration. Customers receive notifications at each stage of the shipment lifecycle.

### 3. Reverse Logistics and Returns Management
End-to-end return handling, including pickup coordination, verification, and restocking for businesses.

### 4. API-Based Integration for Business Clients
Seamless integration for e-commerce platforms and warehouse systems through RESTful APIs for order syncing, tracking, and automated invoicing.

### 5. Warehouse Storage and Inventory Optimization
AI-powered inventory placement and space optimization to reduce turnaround time and improve dispatch efficiency.

## Departments and Responsibilities

### 1. Operations
- Manages pickups, deliveries, and route optimization.  
- Oversees fleet and third-party logistics coordination.  

### 2. Finance
- Handles client billing, invoicing, and claims management.  
- Manages refunds and compensation in line with company policies.  

### 3. Customer Care
- Provides support via email, chat, and phone.  
- Handles customer queries, complaints, and feedback.  
- Coordinates with Operations for escalations.  

### 4. Technical Team
- Maintains system integrations, APIs, and automation pipelines.  
- Oversees data processing, AI model deployment, and infrastructure stability.
