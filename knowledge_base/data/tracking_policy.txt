Tracking Policy:

Tracking numbers are assigned upon booking confirmation.
You can track shipments through:
1. ShipCube dashboard
2. Chatbot / Email Query
3. SMS updates

Tracking Status Stages:
- Order Received
- In Transit
- Out for Delivery
- Delivered

If no tracking update for 48+ hours, contact support@shipcube.com.

Sample Routes & Times:
- Delhi → Mumbai: 2–3 days
- Bengaluru → Chennai: 1–2 days
- Delhi → London: 6–8 days
