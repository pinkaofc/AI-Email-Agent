ShipCube Shipping Policy:

Domestic Delivery:
- Standard: 2–5 business days.
- Express: 1–2 days (additional 5% surcharge).
- Same-day delivery for bookings before 4 PM.

International Delivery:
- Standard: 5–10 business days.
- Express: 3–6 business days.

Packaging Guidelines:
- Fragile items must be labeled and double-packed.
- Liquids must be leak-proof.
- Oversized items may require special handling.

Liability:
- Maximum liability ₹10,000 per shipment unless insured.
- ShipCube not responsible for customs delays or incorrect addresses.
