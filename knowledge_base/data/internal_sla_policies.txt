────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                  INTERNAL SLA POLICIES (OFFICIAL)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This file defines ALL SLA timelines used within ShipCube.
The AI MUST use ONLY these timelines and MUST NOT invent new ones.

────────────────────
1. DELIVERY & TRANSIT SLA
────────────────────

Domestic Shipments:
   • Standard delivery window: 2–7 business days.
   • Maximum investigation window for delays: 24–48 hours.
   • Redelivery attempt SLA: within 1–2 business days after confirmation.

International Shipments:
   • Standard delivery window: 6–14 business days.
   • Customs hold verification SLA: 24–72 hours.
   • Document resubmission SLA: within 12 hours of customer request.

────────────────────
2. ISSUE RESOLUTION SLA
────────────────────

Missing Items:
   • Investigation Start: Immediate (within 4 hours).
   • Warehouse verification: 24–48 hours.
   • Final resolution target: 3–5 business days.

Wrong Delivery:
   • POD verification SLA: 12–24 hours.
   • Courier escalation SLA: 24–48 hours.
   • Redelivery/recovery timeline: 2–4 business days depending on courier.

Damaged Goods:
   • Photo verification: Same business day.
   • Claim review: 1–3 business days.
   • Replacement/next action: After Operations approval.

Delayed Shipment:
   • Movement verification SLA: 12–24 hours.
   • Courier follow-up SLA: 24 hours.
   • Customer update expectation: Every 24 hours until resolved.

Customs Clearance Issue:
   • Document recheck: 6–12 hours.
   • Broker escalation SLA: 24 hours.
   • Clearance timeline (approx): 1–3 working days depending on region.

Billing Errors:
   • Invoice verification SLA: 1–2 business days.
   • Correction issuance: 24–48 hours.

Return Label Request:
   • Label issuance SLA: 24 hours.
   • Pickup scheduling SLA: 24–48 hours.

────────────────────
3. CUSTOMER COMMUNICATION SLA
────────────────────

Email Response SLA:
   • Standard: Within 6–12 hours.
   • Priority / Negative sentiment: Within 2–4 hours.

Follow-up Frequency:
   • If investigation ongoing → update every 24 hours.
   • If courier escalation → update every 48 hours or as courier reports.

────────────────────
4. AI COMMUNICATION BOUNDARIES
────────────────────

The AI MUST:
   • Give SLA windows, not exact days.
   • Say “our team is reviewing within the standard SLA of X–Y hours”.

The AI MUST NOT:
   • Promise fixed timelines like “we’ll fix it today”.
   • State unavailableing internal numbers, IDs, or warehouse details.
