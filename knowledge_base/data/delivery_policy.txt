# ShipCube Delivery & Delay Resolution Policy

## 1. General Delivery Standards
- All domestic orders are shipped within 1–2 business days after confirmation.
- Standard delivery time is 3–7 business days depending on the destination city.
- Orders are dispatched through verified courier partners with live tracking enabled.

## 2. Handling Delays
- If an order is delayed beyond 7 days of dispatch, the customer service team must:
  - Check the tracking status with the courier partner.
  - Inform the customer with an updated delivery estimate.
  - Offer an apology for the inconvenience caused.
  - Provide the tracking ID and courier partner name in the response.

## 3. Customer Communication Guidelines
- Always begin responses with an acknowledgment and apology for the delay.
- Use clear, polite, and empathetic language.
- Example opening line:
  “We sincerely apologize for the delay in your shipment. Our team has checked the status and found that your order is currently in transit.”

## 4. Compensation or Escalation (if applicable)
- If delay exceeds 10 business days:
  - Offer a shipping fee refund or discount on the next purchase.
  - Escalate to the logistics partner’s regional support team.
- If the order is lost, initiate a replacement or full refund within 3 working days.

## 5. Expected Response Template
- Greeting → Apology → Update → Action → Gratitude.
- Example:
  “Thank you for reaching out about your order SC-21323. We apologize for the delay. 
  Our records show it is currently in transit and expected to arrive within 3–5 business days. 
  You can track your shipment using the link below. 
  We appreciate your patience and continued trust in ShipCube.”

## 6. Do Not
- Provide tracking numbers unless confirmed by the courier API.
- Blame courier partners publicly in communications.
- Use technical or internal jargon when replying to customers.
