# ShipCube Refund & Cancellation Policy

## 1. Refund Eligibility
Customers are eligible for a refund under the following circumstances:
1. **Shipment lost in transit:** The courier confirms that the package is missing or untraceable.  
2. **Delivery delay:** For domestic shipments delayed beyond **10 business days** from the dispatch date.  
3. **Damaged goods:** If the customer reports physical damage or missing items within **48 hours** of delivery.

Refunds are subject to verification and adherence to ShipCube’s logistics and claims policies.

## 2. Refund Claim Process
- Customers must raise a refund claim within **7 days** of the issue via:
  - The ShipCube AI Chatbot, or  
  - Email at **support@shipcube.com**  
- Once the claim is received, ShipCube’s logistics team investigates and verifies the issue within **72 hours**.  
- Refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** after successful verification and approval.

Customers will be notified via email once the refund has been initiated.

## 3. Exclusions from Refund Eligibility
Refunds are **not applicable** under the following circumstances:
- Incorrect or incomplete delivery address provided by the sender.  
- Poor or inadequate packaging by the sender leading to damage.  
- Delays caused by **weather conditions**, **courier strikes**, **natural disasters**, or **customs clearance** for international shipments.  
- Shipments without a valid or traceable tracking ID.  
- Cancellations requested after the shipment has been dispatched.

## 4. Refund Method
Approved refunds are credited to:
- The **original mode of payment** used during the booking, or  
- The customer’s **ShipCube Wallet** (for future bookings or credit usage).

Refund confirmation details, including reference number and transaction ID, will be shared via email once processed.

## 5. Cancellations
- Orders can be cancelled **only before dispatch** from the ShipCube warehouse.  
- To cancel, customers must contact **support@shipcube.com** or use the chatbot cancellation option.  
- Once an order is marked as “In Transit,” it cannot be cancelled.  
- Refunds for cancelled shipments follow the same processing time as standard refunds (5–7 business days).

## 6. Additional Notes
- ShipCube reserves the right to decline refund requests that do not comply with the above conditions.  
- Refund timelines may vary slightly based on the customer’s bank or payment gateway policies.  
- For assistance during the refund process, customers can contact the support team between 9 AM and 6 PM IST, Monday to Saturday.

## 7. Missing or Partial Shipments
If a shipment arrives with missing cartons or items, customers should report it within 48 hours.
ShipCube will verify dispatch records and, if confirmed, send replacement stock or process a refund according to the Refund Policy.
