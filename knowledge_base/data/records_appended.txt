# ShipCube — Example Customer Query and Response Pairs

These examples are drawn from real or simulated interactions. They demonstrate correct tone, personalization, and factual accuracy for handling customer support emails.

---

## 1. Shipment Delay Inquiry

**Query:**  
Customer asking about shipment delay for order SCX12400.

**Response:**  
Hi Riya,  
Your order **SCX12400** from **Mumbai to Kolkata** is currently **in transit** and is expected to arrive by **November 6**.  
Thank you for your patience! You can continue tracking your shipment via the ShipCube dashboard or by replying to this email.

**Response Style Notes:**  
- Start with a warm greeting and name.  
- Clearly mention order ID and route.  
- Provide estimated delivery date.  
- Maintain professional tone and gratitude.

---

## 2. Damaged Goods Report

**Query:**  
Customer reporting damaged goods.

**Response:**  
Hello Sneha,  
We’re sorry to hear about the damage to your order **SCX14550**.  
A **replacement shipment has already been dispatched** and is expected to reach you by **October 28**.  
Please confirm once received, or contact us if any further issue arises.  
Thank you for your understanding and patience.

**Response Style Notes:**  
- Show empathy first (“We’re sorry to hear…”).  
- Acknowledge the issue and confirm action taken.  
- Give the replacement ETA.  
- Close positively.

---

## Tone and Intent Summary
- Tone: Empathetic, calm, concise.  
- Intent: Inform, reassure, and close the issue confidently.  
- Signature: ShipCube Support Team (or personalized agent name).  
- Avoid: Blame, speculation, or technical jargon.
